About me

I am a master student at the University of Waterloo studying computer science. I am supervised by Professor Gautam Kamath, who runs the super awesome research group: The Salon. I am also a student affiliate of the Vector Institute.

Before that, I completed my bachelors in computer science in also in UWaterloo when I joined The Salon as an Undergrad Research Assistant. I also worked with Professor Omid Abari on a project that automates the irrigation of plants in a green house using RFID.

You should also check out my photography works!

My research

"My current research primarily focuses on trustworthy machine learning, specifically on how training data and the training process influence a model's parameters and outputs. I am also interested in exploring methods and developing pipelines for unlearning and param editing, in order to increase the robustness of the model."
